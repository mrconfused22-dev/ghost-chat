// PRIVACY ARCHITECTURE

What we store.
What we don't.

🔒

End-to-End Encryption

Private messages are encrypted using NaCl box encryption before they leave your device. The server stores and transmits only ciphertext — completely unreadable without your private key.

TweetNaCl / libsodium
👻

Zero Identity

We never ask for your name, phone number, or email. Your account code is randomly generated. Even we don't know who you are. There is nothing to subpoena.

No KYC ever
💾

Minimal Data Storage

Private messages are stored only in RAM (Redis) and are never written to disk in readable form. Once deleted, they are gone forever with no recovery possible.

Redis in-memory only
⏱️

Auto-Expiring Messages

Private messages: deleted 24 hours after both users read them. Group and World messages: deleted after 7 days. An automated cleanup job runs every hour.

No permanent logs
🛡️

Rate Limiting & Security

All endpoints are rate-limited to prevent brute force attacks. Security headers via Helmet.js. Request body size limits. Input validation on all routes.

Helmet.js + express-rate-limit
🚫

No Ads. Ever.

We do not run ads, sell data to third parties, or use analytics that track you. There is no business model built on your attention or your data.

Zero monetization of data
